About

Dirk was founded
to create the most
fun games ever.

Dirk inherits the harder-edged counterculture and resilience of East Vancouver. Inspired by the artistic trailblazers and authentic creative culture of the area, we aim to elevate and push the creative tech scene of the Pacific Northwest in bold ways while taking great care to be thoughtful stewards of our community.

DK_01

Fun

We are only successful if gamers love the games we make. That means creating the most fun, engaging, compelling experiences possible.

DK_02

Excellence

We are equal part craftspeople and service workers, trying to deliver the best experiences possible to our customers through consistent, transformational improvement in our own technical and artistic skill.

DK_03

Speed

Moving fast means focusing on what's important. Choosing to do what's most important means better games for our players. Speed gives the work urgency. Urgency injects our studio with life.
